Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our thermocouples

Who is Crematory Thermocouple?

www.CrematoryThermocouple.com is owned by National Basic Sensor (NBS). NBS is a manufacturer of thermocouples, RTDs, and other temperature-related accessories. We have been manufacturing for over 100 years and are excited to service the cremation industry.

What is a Thermocouple?

A thermocouple is a temperature measuring device consisting of two dissimilar conductors that form electrical junctions at differing temperatures. They are widely used due to their reliability and accuracy in high-temperature environments.

Will an NBS Thermocouple Work in my Retort?

Yes. Choose between 12", 18", or 24" with a 3/4" NPT Fitting. If you need other specifications consult the factory and we will custom build the thermocouple you need.

What is Included in a Complete Assembly?

A complete assembly consists of an 8 gauge element, inside a mullite protection tube, with a terminal block and connection head.
