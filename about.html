Our Story

A century of innovation in temperature measurement

Our Heritage

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

The story of Crematory Thermocouple begins with our parent company, National Basic Sensor. Since our founder built his first thermocouple in the garage of his Philadelphia row house in the early 1900's, we have strived to achieve the utmost in customer satisfaction.

For over a century, we've been at the forefront of temperature measurement technology, constantly refining our processes and expertise. As our industry knowledge grew, we recognized the specialized needs of the crematory industry, which led to the creation of Crematory Thermocouple as a focused division dedicated to serving retort furnace applications with precision and reliability.

Today, we combine our rich heritage of craftsmanship with modern manufacturing techniques to deliver thermocouples that meet the exacting standards required in crematory operations. Our commitment to quality, precision, and customer service remains as strong as it was when our founder handcrafted his first temperature measurement device.
